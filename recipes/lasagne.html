Lasagne

Lasagne is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with rich meat sauce,
creamy b√©chamel, and cheese, then baked to perfection. It's comforting, flavorful,
and perfect for a family meal or special occasions.

ingredients:

meat Sauce
  • Olive oil
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 500g beef or pork
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • salt and pepper
Bechamel/Cheese sauce
  • 500ml milk
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 tablespoons of flour
  • pinch of nutmeg
  • salt and pepper
Other ingredients
  • lasagne sheets
  • grated mozzarella cheese
  • grated parmesan cheese

instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce:
    1. heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat, cook onion and garlic until soft.
    2. once onions and garlic are soft, add meat and cook until browned.
    3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano,basil,salt, and pepper
    4. simmer for another 20 minutes, stir occasionally.
  2. Make the Bechamel sauce
    1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat
    2. add flour, whisk until butter and flour are mixed and no longer smell like flour
    3. Gradually pour in milk, whisk constantly to avoid lumps
    4. Cook until the sauce thickens, then season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  3. Assemble the lasagne
    1. Preheat oven to 180deg
    2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce to the bottom of the baking dish
    3. add a layer of lasagne sheet, follow by bechamel, then sauce, then repeat
    4. finish by placing a final sheet and then bechamel sauce
    5. sprinkle mozzarella and parmesan cheese on top layer
  4. bake:
    1. Cover the dish with aluminum foil to prevent drying out, and bake for 25m
    2. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20mins or until golden and bubbling.
    3. remove from the oven and allow to rest 10 minutes before eating..
